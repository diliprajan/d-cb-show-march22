dilip + Carrom Board Show Recap

On Friday 3/25/2022, dilip & Carrom Board performed for a sold-out audience of fans at The Delancey in the Lower East Side of NYC. (~200 tickets sold)

The duo was backed by a full band and played through a 45 minute set of original songs ranging from indie rock to R&B.

The setlist included fan-favorite, Pavements (75k+ Spotify streams) and Ghost Donkey (30k+ Spotify streams).

More about the artists

Carrom Board is the indie music project of Prashant Ramesh. His influences include Tame Impala, Still Woozy, and Mac Demarco. (Spotify)

dilip is an indie R&B artist based in Brooklyn. His influences include Frank Ocean, Cautious Clay, and Daniel Caesar. (Spotify)
